## Week 4: the decision

Apply `ads/thresholds.md` mechanically. Circle one:

- **BUILD** - >=300 waitlist or >=10 kit sales. Start building the full box;
  announce founding price window to the list.
- **ITERATE** - 100-300 waitlist. The pull exists but the message or persona
  is off. Change ONE major variable (headline promise, lead persona, or
  offer framing) and run 2 more weeks.
- **KILL / PIVOT** - <100 waitlist with budget spent and 25+ videos posted.
  The demand is not there at testable cost. Write the post-mortem below,
  keep the list warm, pivot the promise or shelve.
