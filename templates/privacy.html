Privacy

Short version: we collect your email address because you asked us to, we count visits without cookies, and we only set a cookie if you say yes to it.

What we collect

  • Your email address, when you join the waitlist. It is stored with our email provider (MailerLite) and used to send you {{ brand.name }} updates. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing deletes you from the list.
  • Anonymous visit counts, via GoatCounter, which does not use cookies and does not identify you.
  • Ad measurement, via the Meta pixel, only if you accept the cookie notice. This tells us whether our adverts led to signups. Decline it and the site works identically.

What we never do

  • Sell or share your email with anyone.
  • Track you across other sites ourselves.
  • Keep your data after you ask us to remove it.

Payments

If you buy a digital product, payment is processed by Payhip, who act as the merchant of record. We never see your card details.

Your rights

Under UK and EU data protection law you can ask for a copy of your data, correct it, or have it deleted. Email us at hello@{{ brand.domain }} and it will be done within 30 days.
