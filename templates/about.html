About this index

Each week (except during December) the Department of Public Service and Administration publishes the Public Service Vacancy Circular: a single, long PDF listing every advertised post across national and provincial government. It is comprehensive, but hard to search, and heavy to open on a phone or a slow connection.

This site republishes those same vacancies in a lighter form. We read the latest circular, separate it into individual posts, and present each one as a page you can search, filter and open quickly. The pages are plain and static, so they load on modest connections and work even with JavaScript switched off.

Please note

This is an independent, unofficial index. We do not receive applications and we are not the employer. Every advert names the department to apply to, a closing date, and a reference number: apply directly to that department, by that date, quoting that reference. Where anything here differs from the official circular, the circular is correct.

The source is the DPSA newsroom: dpsa.gov.za/newsroom/psvc.
