# Series 01: permission myths (PM)

Six scripts that bust the gatekeeping myths. This series does the brand's core
job: create the feeling "oh, I could actually do this". Post these early and
often (see `../calendar.md`).

Voice reminders for this series: state the fact plainly, no triumphalism, no
"they don't want you to know" conspiracy tone. On-screen text never uses emoji.
All on-screen text in ink `#221F1C` on plaster `#F1E9DE` cards, terracotta
`#C4602F` for the single accent word. See `../workflow.md` for text conventions.

---

## PM-01: the degree myth

**Hook** (on-screen, first 1.5s): You do not need a design degree

**Length:** 35s

**VO script**
> Here is a fact the industry does not advertise.
> In the UK, interior design is not a protected profession.
> There is no licence. There is no legal exam.
> Anyone can charge for residential design work. Today.
> Architects are regulated. Interior designers are not.
> So what do you actually need?
> A trained eye. A simple process. And one client who trusts you.
> The eye, you already have.
> The process can be learned in weeks, not years.
> The only real gate is starting.
> We are building a kit for exactly that. The waitlist is in our bio.

**Visuals** (CapCut, shot by shot)
1. 0.0-1.5s: full-screen text card, hook in large ink text on plaster. The word "degree" in terracotta.
2. 1.5-8s: slow-pan stock clip of a calm living room (Pexels search: "scandinavian living room pan"). Overlay text: "Not a protected profession in the UK".
3. 8-15s: stock clip of hands flipping paint swatches (Pexels: "paint swatches hands"). Overlay: "No licence. No legal exam."
4. 15-24s: stock clip of a laptop with a moodboard on screen, or your own Canva screen recording. Overlay: "A trained eye + a simple process".
5. 24-32s: stock clip of someone writing at a kitchen table (Pexels: "writing notebook coffee table"). Overlay: "Learned in weeks, not years".
6. 32-35s: closing card on plaster: "Maison Method. Waitlist in bio." (ink text, no emoji).

**Caption**
The fact nobody says out loud: interior design is not a legally protected profession in the UK. You can charge for residential and e-design work without a degree. The gate is not real. #interiordesign #sidehustle #interiordesignuk #careerchange
