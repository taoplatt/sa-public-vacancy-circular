# 30-day posting calendar

One to two posts per day. All 30 scripts (PM, MT, TR, FC, BM series) are
scheduled in the first 26 days; the remaining slots are "re-hook" slots, where
you re-cut a proven winner with a new hook (see the weekly review ritual
below). Day 1 is a Monday; shift the grid to whatever Monday you start.

## The strategy in one paragraph

Front-load permission myths (PM) and transformations (TR): they are the reach
engines, they need no trust to land, and together they establish the account's
two registers (permission and aesthetics). Introduce money transparency (MT)
in week two, once viewers have a reason to believe you are not selling hype.
Hold the first client path (FC) until week three; "here is the exact message"
lands far better after the account has been giving freely for a fortnight.
Weave behind the method (BM) throughout from day one, roughly twice a week, so
the waitlist always has a live reason to exist.

## Best UK posting times

- Weekday evenings 19:00-21:00 are the primary slot (commute done, sofa time).
- Weekday lunch 12:00-13:00 is the secondary slot for double-post days.
- Weekday early 07:00-08:30 works for commuter scrolling; use it for
  punchy PM scripts.
- Weekends: one post, 09:30-11:00, when home-improvement mood peaks.
- These are starting assumptions. After two weeks, TikTok Studio's follower
  activity chart outranks any general advice; adjust to your own graph.

Re-hook slots are not reposts of the same file. Same b-roll and VO body, new
first 1.5 seconds (new hook text, new opening line). TikTok treats it as a new
video; you learn which hook carried the performance.

## Weekly review ritual (Sunday, 20 minutes)

1. Open `hooks-tracker.md` and fill in 72-hour numbers for every post that is
   at least 72 hours old.
2. Give each a verdict: kill, iterate or scale (rules are in the tracker).
3. Pick next week's re-hook slots from the "scale" and "iterate" rows.
4. Write 2 new hook variants for each chosen video before closing the laptop;
   Sunday-you is a better copywriter than Thursday-night-you.
5. Every second Sunday (days 14 and 28): flag the top 3 hooks by 72-hour views
   as Meta ad creative candidates and note them for the `ads/` folder work.
6. Check the follower activity chart in TikTok Studio and nudge posting times
   if your audience clearly peaks elsewhere.

## Rules of the month

- Never skip a day to polish a video. A rough post outranks a perfect draft
  (see the "post, do not agonise" rule in `workflow.md`).
- Reply to every substantive comment within 24 hours; comments in hour one
  matter most, so post when you can lurk for 20 minutes after.
- BM-06 and any milestone post: only when the number is real, no rounding up.
- If a post flops, it cost you nothing. The tracker converts flops into
  information; that is the entire model.
